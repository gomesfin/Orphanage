Who Are We?

Africa Home Haven Foundation is a non-profit organization founded by Dr. Samson Ghebremariam, a prominent scientist and leader within the global pharmaceutical industry. As a native of Northern Ethiopia, he understands the struggles orphaned children face due to conflict, poverty, and disease. He has therefore established this foundation in order to help alleviate the challenges faced these vulnerable children

Our mission is to provide a loving home, education, healthcare, and a bright future for orphaned children in Ethiopia, Kenya, and South Sudan.
